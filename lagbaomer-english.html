The Holy Day

Lag BaOmer in Meron

Lag BaOmer marks the Hilula — the anniversary of the passing of the Holy Tannah, Rebbe Shimon Bar Yochai. It is the most powerful day of the year for prayer and divine blessing.

Hundreds of thousands of Jews make the journey to the northern Galilean town of Meron to pour out their hearts in prayer at the holy Tzion of the Rashbi.

Tzidkat Rashbi brings thousands of kvitlach and prayer requests from across the globe to the holy Tzion. Tzidkat Rashbi also sponsors special learning programs for study of Rashbi's Holy Zohar throughout the year.

"The Holy Rashbi pledged happiness and joy to all who would contribute to the happiness of his day — and may His merit be a blessing for you the whole year."

Witnessed Miracles

01

A Shidduch Found

A father prayed for his daughter at the Tzion on Lag BaOmer. Within the year she found her match. The Rashbi's merit brought salvation to their home.

02

A Son Recovered

Parents begged for healing for their sick child at the holy resting place. Their son made a full and complete recovery — a miracle witnessed by all.

03

Parnassah Restored

A man on the verge of despair, unable to provide for his family, prayed at the Rashbi. Shortly after he was offered management of a successful company.

04

Children Granted

Couples who had longed for children came to Meron with heartfelt prayer. Time and again their prayers were answered through the merit of Reb Shimon.
